Our Mission

This NGO is dedicated to providing a comprehensive platform and support to underprivileged minorities, with the goal of amplifying their voices and promoting social justice.

This organization focuses on communities that have historically been marginalized and excluded, including ethnic minorities, refugees, people with disabilities and LGBTQ+ communities.


The main objective of "Voces Unidas" is to empower these minorities through educational programs, legal advice and economic development opportunities.

The NGO organizes workshops and seminars that address issues such as human rights, social inclusion and community development, fostering self-management and leadership within these communities.


In addition, we work in collaboration with other organizations and governmental entities to influence public policies that benefit minorities.

The NGO also carries out campaigns to raise awareness and defend human rights, using various media platforms to give visibility to its activities.
